# Open Graph Image Instructions
## "The Illuminated Journey" — ScriptureHub Share Graphics

This folder (`public/`) is where your two share images must be saved.
Vite copies everything in `public/` directly into `dist/` when you build.
That means your images will be live at your domain root automatically.

---

## Files to create and save here:

| Filename | Size | Platform |
|----------|------|----------|
| `og-image.jpg` | 1200 × 630 px | Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Telegram |
| `og-square.jpg` | 1024 × 1024 px | WhatsApp, iMessage |

---

## Design Brief — "The Illuminated Journey"

### Core Concept
Ancient Truth, Digitally Activated.

### Scene Description
- **Background colour:** Midnight Navy (`#0D1B2A`)
- **Foreground subject:** An open, weathered Study Bible resting on a dark surface
- **Interaction:** A stylized modern smartphone hovering above the Bible
- **Light effect:** Streams of Radiant Gold (`#C9A84C`) and Ivory White (`#F5F0E8`)
  light pour from the phone screen down onto the Bible pages
- **Discovery Icons:** Where the light touches, small icons gently lift off the pages:
  - 👑 Crown (Royalty of Scripture)
  - 🧭 Compass (Guidance)
  - 📜 Scroll (Ancient Word)
- **Mood:** Mysterious, sacred, high-tech

### Branding Elements
- **Top Right:** ScriptureHub logo mark + tagline: "Discover the Joy in Every Verse."
- **Bottom Right:** "Curated by Silas Clergy"
- **Font style:** Playfair Display for any text overlays

## How to Create This Image

### Option 1 — Canva (Free, Recommended)
1. Go to canva.com → Create design → Custom size
2. Set 1200 × 630 px for the wide version
3. Use a dark navy background
4. Add Bible and phone graphics (search Canva elements)
5. Add a golden glow effect using warm yellow shapes with blur
6. Add your text in Playfair Display font
7. Download as JPG → save as `og-image.jpg` in this folder
8. Repeat at 1024 × 1024 px → save as `og-square.jpg`

### Option 2 — Adobe Express (Free tier available)
Same steps as Canva — wider selection of spiritual/book imagery.

### Option 3 — Commission a designer
Share this brief with a designer on Fiverr or Upwork.
Budget: $15–$40 for both sizes.
